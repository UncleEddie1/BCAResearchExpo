Sponsorship

The BCA Research Expo is sponsored by the Bergen County Academies, a public magnet school of choice, located in Hackensack, New Jersey. The Expo relies on donations from individuals and corporations who promote and support scientific discovery and who aim to promote STEM education for students at all levels.Top of page
